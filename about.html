About Duece

Duece is a dedicated mixed-martial-arts instructor based in Seguin, Texas. He offers one-on-one and small-group training sessions covering Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Boxing, Muay Thai, and MMA fundamentals. His focus is building real-world technique, endurance, and discipline in a supportive team environment.

Whether you’re stepping onto the mats for the first time or honing your competitive edge, Duece’s hands-on training approach is crafted to develop skill, confidence, and respect for the arts.

Private sessions by appointment only.
